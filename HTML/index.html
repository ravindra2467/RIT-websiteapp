About RIT Institute

RIT Institute, Andhra Pradesh (RIT AP), also known as Ravindra Institute of Technology, Amaravati, is a private institute located near Neerukonda village in Mangalagiri mandal of Guntur district in Andhra Pradesh, India, in the area of the planned capital city Amaravati. The institute was established in 2017 by the Ravindra Trust through the Andhra Pradesh government.

We produce creative thinkers, thought leaders, and social workers with deep subject-area expertise. The uniqueness of the school lies in its potential to equip students to deal with real-world problems through rich learning experiences.
